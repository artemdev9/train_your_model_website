Handwritten Digit Recognition

Drawing Digits:

On the main page, you will find a canvas labeled TRAIN YOUR MODEL

To draw a digit (0-9), select the digit you want to draw from the number displayed at the top. Initially, it shows 0.

Use your mouse or touch input to draw the digit within the canvas.

Click the Save button to save your drawing as a training sample for the selected digit.

The Clear button can be used to clear the canvas if you make a mistake.

Training the Model:

After collecting a sufficient number of training samples (ideally around 100 for each digit), click the Train Model button on the right side.

The model will be trained using the collected data. The training progress will be displayed in the console.

After training is complete, the model can be used to predict digits.
