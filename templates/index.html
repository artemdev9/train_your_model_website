Welcome to the TRAIN YOUR MODEL WEBSITE! Explore the world of AI, create your neural network, and learn firsthand the impact of data quality on AI accuracy.

Drawing Digits:

On the main page, you will find a two canvasses. The top canvas is used to make the samples to train the model.

Use your mouse or touch input to draw the numbers shown to the right of the top canvas on the canvas.

Click the Save button to save your drawing as a training sample for the selected digit.

The Clear button can be used to clear the canvas if you make a mistake.

Training the Model:

After collecting a sufficient number of training samples (MORE THAN 10), click the Train Model button on the right side.

NOTE: You can add as many samples as you like, they all add up. This is especially useful if you are getting bad results in the prediction section.

The model will be trained using the collected data.

After training is complete you will get an alert, then the model can be used to predict digits.

Predicting Digits:

Scroll down to the Prediction section, the lower section of the web app.

You will find another canvas, the bottom canvas

Draw a digit on this canvas using your mouse or touch input.

Click the Predict button to see the model's prediction for the drawn digit.

The predicted digit will be displayed below the button.
